🌺 Hibiscus

🌿 Scientific Name

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

💧 Watering

Hibiscus loves moisture. Water regularly to keep the soil evenly moist, especially during summer. Avoid waterlogging the roots.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sun (6–8 hours/day). In very hot climates, partial shade during the hottest part of the day is helpful.

🌱 Soil

Well-draining, slightly acidic soil enriched with organic compost is ideal.

🌸 Flowering

Produces beautiful large flowers in various colors. Regular pruning and fertilizing help promote blooms.

🧪 Care Tips

  • Prune regularly to encourage bushy growth and more flowers.
  • Use a balanced liquid fertilizer every 2–3 weeks during growing season.
  • Protect from frost and harsh winds.
  • Watch for pests like aphids and whiteflies.
